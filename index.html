A Hacker's Guide to NYC

Inspired by A Hacker's guide to the Bay Area, this guide is intended for "hackers" new to New York or wishing to explore more of the tech scene in the city. I write from the perspective of a 20-something female who works from home, enjoys learning from new friends at hackathons and meetups, and has interests outside of tech.
